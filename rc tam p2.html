Movie Name:Alaipayuthey 2000

Director: Maniratnam

Overview: Alaipayuthey--- Overview
The film follows Karthik, a carefree software engineer, and Shakthi, a dedicated medical student, who fall in love despite their different family backgrounds. When their parents disapprove of their relationship, they secretly marry and begin a life together, hoping their families will eventually accept them. However, the reality of marriage brings conflicts and misunderstandings, straining their bond. When Shakthi’s father passes away, their relationship worsens, leading to emotional distance. Meanwhile, Karthik works to restore Shakthi’s sister’s broken engagement, but a misunderstanding further damages their marriage. Just as Shakthi realizes her mistake and rushes to reconcile, she meets with an accident, falling into a coma. Karthik searches desperately for her and, upon finding her, acknowledges his shortcomings as a husband. In the end, Shakthi wakes up, and the two reconcile, reaffirming their love.
