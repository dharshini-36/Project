Movie Name:Saripodha Sanivaaram 2024

Director: Vivek Athreya

Overview: Sanipodha Sanivaaram -- Overview Surya, an insurance agent with severe anger issues, channels his rage only on Saturdays, following a promise to his late mother. He reconnects with his childhood love, Charulatha, a police constable aiding the oppressed residents of Sokulapalem. Corrupt CI Daya terrorizes the community while plotting against his rival, Koormanand. Surya, revealed as the mysterious "Saturday vigilante," disrupts Daya’s plans but is forced to abandon his violent rule after saving his family and earning the trust of Sokulapalem’s people. When Daya frames the vigilante for Koormanand’s murder, the residents rise against him, leading to his demise. Surya then marries Charulatha as peace is restored.
