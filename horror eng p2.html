Movie Name:The Conjuring 2 2016

Director: James Wan

Overview: The Conjuring 2 (2016) - Overview
In 1976, paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren examine the Amityville murders and encounter a demonic nun, forewarning Ed’s possible death. A year later, the Hodgson family in Enfield, London, experiences terrifying supernatural events, with young Janet becoming possessed by the spirit of Bill Wilkins. The Warrens are called to verify the haunting but initially doubt its authenticity. However, they soon realize that Wilkins is merely a pawn of the true demon—Valak, the nun. Lorraine discovers the demon’s name, using it to banish Valak and save Janet. The Warrens return home, adding another eerie artifact to their collection.
