Movie Name:83 2021

Director: Kabir khan

Overview: 83--- Overview
This sports drama captures India's historic underdog victory in the 1983 Cricket World Cup. The film follows Kapil Dev and his team as they defy expectations to claim cricket’s biggest prize. Initially dismissed as underdogs, India starts strong by defeating West Indies but soon stumbles with heavy losses. When all hope seems lost, Kapil Dev’s record-breaking 175* against Zimbabwe turns the tide. Gaining momentum, India defeats England in the semi-finals, setting up a thrilling final against the West Indies. Defending a modest 183, the Indian bowlers shine, with Dev’s iconic catch of Viv Richards changing the game. India pulls off a stunning victory, igniting celebrations across the nation. The film ends with real footage of Kapil Dev recalling the triumph, cementing this moment as a defining chapter in Indian sports history.
